Note: Ridge length is calculated as 13 bays × 93 m = 1,209 m. Eaves perimeter is calculated as 2 × 93 m + 2 × 13 × 8.83 m = 186 + 229.58 = 415.58 m. The surveyed building perimeter (464.581 m) is used as the basis for wall flashing quantities. All purlin quantities are derived from total sloped roof area divided by purlin spacing. Downpipe count is based on 15 m maximum spacing along eaves. Quantities exclude roof sheeting panels; a separate schedule should be prepared for primary roof sheets.
